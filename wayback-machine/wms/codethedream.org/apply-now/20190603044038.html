Apply Now

Who can apply?  We provide a pathway to a tech career for people who might not otherwise have the opportunity, particularly minority and immigrant young people, ages 15+.  But regardless of your background, we’d love to hear from you! Just fill out the simple application form below.

When and where are our classes?  Code the Dream holds classes throughout the year. We are based in North Carolina, but you don’t have to be! We offer fully remote classes to students outside of North Carolina.

Note: Students with previous coding experience can apply directly to CTD Labs. Please describe your experience in the application below.
